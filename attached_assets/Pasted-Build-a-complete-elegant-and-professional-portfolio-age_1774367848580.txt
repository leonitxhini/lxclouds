Build a complete, elegant, and professional portfolio/agency website for "LX CLOUDS" 
(domain: lxclouds.com). This is both a personal brand and a company through which 
the owner runs client projects — primarily building web apps and websites. The site 
serves as a showcase to attract new clients by displaying previously built apps and 
websites as live previews or visual case studies.

BRAND IDENTITY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
- Name: LX CLOUDS
- Domain: lxclouds.com
- Tone: Serious, elegant, modern, trustworthy — like a boutique digital studio
- Aesthetic: Dark theme with deep charcoal/near-black backgrounds and a rich, 
  sophisticated green as the primary accent color
- COLOR PALETTE (strictly follow these):
    • Background primary:   #0A0F0A  (deep dark green-black)
    • Background secondary: #0F1A0F  (slightly lighter dark green-black)
    • Surface / cards:      #111D11  (dark forest tone for cards/glass)
    • Primary accent:       #00C46A  (vibrant emerald green — CTAs, highlights)
    • Secondary accent:     #1DB954  (slightly warmer green for hover states)
    • Subtle glow/aura:     #00FF7F  (neon mint — used sparingly for glows only)
    • Text primary:         #F0FFF4  (near-white with a cool green tint)
    • Text secondary:       #8FA98F  (muted sage — for subtitles, meta text)
    • Border/divider:       #1F331F  (dark green border, barely visible)
- Feeling: Like a premium fintech or cybersecurity studio — dark, clean, precise
- Typography: 
    • Headings: "Cormorant Garamond" or "Playfair Display" — refined and elegant
    • Body: "DM Sans" or "Sora" — clean and modern
    • Accent/labels: "JetBrains Mono" — for tags, code-style labels, small caps

VISUAL STYLE DETAILS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
- All sections sit on very dark green-black backgrounds
- Cards and panels use a frosted glassmorphism effect with a dark green tint:
    background: rgba(0, 196, 106, 0.05);
    border: 1px solid rgba(0, 196, 106, 0.15);
    backdrop-filter: blur(12px);
- Glow effects on hover: box-shadow: 0 0 24px rgba(0, 255, 127, 0.15)
- Gradient text for the logo and main headline:
    background: linear-gradient(135deg, #00C46A, #00FF7F);
    -webkit-background-clip: text;
    -webkit-text-fill-color: transparent;
- Subtle green noise/grain texture overlay on the hero background (5% opacity)
- Floating particle/orb animation in the hero: soft glowing green orbs drifting
  slowly, like clouds — ties into the "LX CLOUDS" brand name beautifully
- Section dividers: thin 1px lines using the border color #1F331F

DESIGN DO's AND DON'Ts

✅ Consistent use of the exact green palette defined above
✅ Glassmorphism cards with green-tinted borders
✅ Subtle glows and particle animations tied to the "clouds" theme
✅ Generous breathing room and whitespace between sections
✅ Monospace font for tags and small technical labels
❌ No bright white backgrounds anywhere
❌ No blue, purple, or mixed accent colors

❌ No overcrowded layouts or cheap-looking gradients
❌ No clipart, emoji, or generic icons
